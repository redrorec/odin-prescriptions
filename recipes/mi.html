Acute Myocardial Infarction

Simple steps in the initial management of MI

According to the ACLS protocol we should be able to very easily identify a MI through admissional ECG and typical Hx.

Initial intervention

    Cardiac continous monitoring 12-lead ECG IV access
    NPO NS 0.9% 500 mL EV Morphine 10mg + Distilled water 9 mL. Administer 2-5mg IV immediately. Repeat every 5 minutes if needed. Aspirin 325mg PO now. Then 100mg PO 24/24h. Ticagrelor 180mg VO. Then 90mg PO 12/12h until 1y after admission for MI.
