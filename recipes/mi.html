Acute Myocardial Infarction

Simple steps in the initial management of MI

According to the ACLS protocol we should be able to very easily identify a MI through admissional ECG and typical Hx.

Initial intervention

  • Cardiac continous monitoring
  • 12-lead ECG
  • IV access
  1. NPO
  2. NS 0.9% 500 mL EV
  3. Morphine 10mg + Distilled water 9 mL. Administer 2-5mg IV immediately. Repeat every 5 minutes if needed.
  4. Aspirin 325mg PO now. Then 100mg PO 24/24h.
  5. Ticagrelor 180mg VO. Then 90mg PO 12/12h until 1y after admission for MI.
